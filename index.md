# Director's Guide to AI Governance OKF Bundle

This knowledge bundle represents the structured concepts, operating model guidance, and case studies from the AICD/HTI Director's Guide to AI Governance (June 2026).

## Governance

* [ontology.md](ontology.md) — Single source of truth: type registry, relationship taxonomy, tag vocabulary

## Sections

* [Foundations](foundations/) — Core concepts, roles, duties, and regulatory landscape of AI, including the Privacy Act ADM disclosure duty (Appendix A).
* [Operating Model](operating-model/) — Strategy, structure, practices, enablers, and measuring AI returns (Part 3).
* [Case Studies](case-studies/) — CBA, Atlassian, Canteen, Westpac, and Telstra.
* [Checklists & Reference](checklists/) — SME/NFP checklist (Appendix C), resources (Appendix B), and glossary (Appendix D).
* [Errata](errata/) — Audited errors in the source Guide (Hard-Error Register) and an exhaustive Source Coverage Ledger tracking bundle completeness against the source. Read alongside any privacy/ADM-related concept.
* [Deliverables](deliverables/) — Client-facing outputs for paid Board AI Oversight Workshops: the live diagnostic artifact (`board-workshop-diagnostic.html`), the facilitator run-of-show, and the commercial offer one-pager. The diagnostic artifact is a registered Deliverable Parity Contract (see [ontology.md](ontology.md) §Deliverable Parity Contracts) — its embedded snapshot must be kept in sync with `foundations/privacy-act-adm-disclosure.md`, `errata/privacy-act-hard-error-register.md`, `foundations/regulatory-obligations-appendix-a.md`, and `foundations/board-role-regulatory-landscape.md`. Privacy Act legal facts also fall under §Cross-Bundle Authority Contracts (`privacy-act-okf-adm`).

## Provenance

This bundle was canonically re-ingested on 2026-07-03 directly from the source PDF and
its extracted text (56 pages), audited against `directors-guide-hard-error-register.md`,
with a same-day remediation pass after an independent review found the first pass had
missed several recurring content blocks (see [ontology.md](ontology.md) v0.4 and the
[Source Coverage Ledger](errata/source-coverage-ledger.md)).

**Privacy Act APP 1.7–1.9 legal authority (from 2026-07-13):** statutory facts in this
bundle track the companion `privacy-act-okf` bundle via ontology §Cross-Bundle Authority
Contracts (`privacy-act-okf-adm`). Do **not** restore the Guide's p.39 ADM summary, GDPR
Art 22 threshold wording, or a conflated s13G-only penalty story as this bundle's own
position — see [Hard-Error Register](errata/privacy-act-hard-error-register.md) and
[Privacy Act ADM Disclosure Duty](foundations/privacy-act-adm-disclosure.md). See ontology.md
version history for the full change record.
