# Director's Guide to AI Governance OKF Bundle

This knowledge bundle represents the structured concepts, operating model guidance, and case studies from the AICD/HTI Director's Guide to AI Governance (June 2026).

## Governance

* [ontology.md](ontology.md) — Single source of truth: type registry, relationship taxonomy, tag vocabulary

## Sections

* [Foundations](foundations/) — Core concepts, roles, duties, and regulatory landscape of AI, including the Privacy Act ADM disclosure duty (Appendix A).
* [Operating Model](operating-model/) — Strategy, structure, practices, enablers, and measuring AI returns (Part 3).
* [Case Studies](case-studies/) — CBA, Atlassian, Canteen, Westpac, and Telstra.
* [Checklists & Reference](checklists/) — SME/NFP checklist (Appendix C), resources (Appendix B), and glossary (Appendix D).
* [Errata](errata/) — Audited errors in the source Guide (Hard-Error Register) and an exhaustive Source Coverage Ledger tracking bundle completeness against the source. Read alongside any privacy/ADM-related concept.
