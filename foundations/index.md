# Foundations

## Concepts

* [AI Opportunities and Risks](opportunities-and-risks.md) — AI systems offer significant productivity benefits but introduce unique commercial, reputational, regulatory, and human risks.
* [AI is a Transformative Technology](transformative-technology.md) — Developments in AI technology, including agentic, generative, and traditional AI, are advancing at an unprecedented rate and reshaping how organisations operate.
* [Privacy Act ADM Disclosure Duty](privacy-act-adm-disclosure.md) — From 10 December 2026, privacy policies must disclose the kinds of personal information, and kinds of solely- and substantially-assisted decisions, made by a computer program with a significant effect on an individual.
* [The Role of the Board and the Regulatory Landscape](board-role-regulatory-landscape.md) — Board-level oversight of AI is part of directors' existing duties, requiring compliance with privacy, consumer protection, and other legal frameworks.
