# Operating Model

## Concepts

* [AI Governance Enablers](governance-enablers.md) — Successful AI adoption relies on tech infrastructure, a culture of responsible AI, and proactive stakeholder engagement.
* [AI Governance Practices](governance-practices.md) — Boards must oversee how AI risks are identified and managed through existing frameworks, including vendor risk and cyber security.
* [AI Governance Structure](governance-structure.md) — The board must map AI responsibilities across the organisation and build board-level AI literacy to ensure effective oversight.
* [AI Strategy Alignment](strategy.md) — The board should ensure that AI investments align with the organisation's strategy, values, and established risk appetite.
* [Measuring Value and Returns from AI](measuring-ai-returns.md) — Part 3 of the Guide: boards should oversee AI ROI at the level of specific, measurable use cases, testing adoption, translation efficiency, and accuracy across the experimentation, integration and scaling phases.
