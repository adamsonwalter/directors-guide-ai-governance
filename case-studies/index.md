# Case Studies

## Concepts

* [Case Study: Atlassian's Responsible Tech Review](atlassian-case-study.md) — Atlassian's iterative design of a Responsible Tech Review Template to ensure product teams assess AI risks.
* [Case Study: Building Westpac Senior Leadership's AI Literacy](westpac-case-study.md) — Westpac's Executive AI Council and a sequenced board AI education series — culture and transformation, then responsible AI, then technical grounding — build a shared board/executive baseline understanding of AI.
* [Case Study: Canteen – Listening to the Voice of Members](canteen-case-study.md) — Canteen's youth-majority board established a 12-month, time-bound AI Sprint Committee to set guardrails and integrate AI oversight into existing governance while consulting members.
* [Case Study: Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA)](cba-case-study.md) — CBA's board holds the CEO and executive leadership accountable for AI risk under its Risk Management Framework, overseen through a Model Risk Governance Committee and a dedicated AI Risk Committee.
* [Case Study: Telstra – AI Governance Operating Model in Action](telstra-case-study.md) — Telstra's application of all four components of HTI's AI Governance Operating Model — strategy folded into corporate strategy, a redesigned AI Risk Oversight Council, plain-English policy simplification, and mandatory Copilot/Responsible AI training.
