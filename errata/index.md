# Errata

Documented errors and gaps in *A Director's Guide to AI Governance* (AICD/HTI, v2, June
2026). Two different kinds of audit live here: whether what the bundle says is *correct*
(errata) and whether the bundle *captured everything* the source contains (coverage).

## Errata

* [Hard-Error Register — Privacy Act / ADM Disclosure Content in the Guide](privacy-act-hard-error-register.md) — Seven audited errors and gaps in the Guide's treatment of the Privacy Act automated-decision-making disclosure duty, graded CONFIRMED / UNSETTLED / REASONED VIEW against the canonical Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) position as at 2 July 2026.

## Coverage Ledgers

* [Source Coverage Ledger — A Director's Guide to AI Governance](source-coverage-ledger.md) — Exhaustive inventory of every named structural unit (Box/Table/Figure/callout/appendix/case) in the 56-page source, mapped to the bundle file that captures it, with a Status column — the mechanical check that a second AI review caught and a sequential re-read did not.
