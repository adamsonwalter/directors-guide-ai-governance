CONFIRMED Statutory tort — serious invasions of privacy (in force 10 Jun 2025). Individuals only may sue; intentional or reckless conduct (not mere negligence); damages capped at the greater of $478,550 or the defamation non-economic-loss cap; 1-year-from-awareness / 3-year limitation. Relevant to biometric, facial-recognition or covert-monitoring deployments.
CONFIRMED Director stepping-stone liability — s180 Corporations Act. ASIC Report 798 (Oct 2024): directors must discharge care and diligence duties over AI adoption, deployment and use, and be aware of foreseeable risk. Not having an inventory is itself evidence of the gap — ignorance is not a defence to s180 once the risk is foreseeable and known industry-wide.
